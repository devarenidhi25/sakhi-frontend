How SAKHI Helps You

PROCESS

How It Works

1

Choose Your Reporting Method

Decide whether to report anonymously or with your identity, based on your comfort level.

2

Share Your Experience

Fill out the form with details about the incident. Include as much information as you feel comfortable sharing.

3

Submit your Story

Submit your story to receive guidance and support. Our team will connect you with trusted resources and organizations that can help.

4

Generate and download your form

After submitting, a report will be generated for you. You can download it for your records and use it for further action if needed.
